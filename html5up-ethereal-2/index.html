About Us

Our yakitori shop offers you an intimate culinary journey centered around grilled skewered delicacies. With origins tracing back to street vendors, our modern establishment focuses on expertly grilling chicken parts to perfection. Patrons savor a range of flavors and textures, from succulent thigh meat to crispy skin. The ambiance is often rustic yet cozy, encouraging conviviality. Accompanied by a selection of drinks, including sake and beer, our shop provides a gastronomic adventure that celebrates the simplicity and artistry of skewered cuisine in the heart of Japanese culinary tradition.

Our Spirits

Pairing yakitori with Japanese alcohol creates a harmonious culinary experience. The smoky flavors of grilled skewers complement the nuanced tastes of sake, enhancing the umami notes in both. Crisp and clean lagers like Sapporo or Asahi provide a refreshing contrast to the savory and charred elements of yakitori. For those seeking a stronger option, shochu can balance the richness of the grilled meats. Whichever choice, the combination showcases the intricate interplay of flavors, elevating both the food and the drink to create a memorable dining adventure.

Our Food

Seasonal yakitori reflects Japan's changing flavors. Spring might bring cherry blossom-infused skewers, while autumn features mushrooms. These variations capture the essence of each season in delectable bites.
